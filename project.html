Dronacharya

Dronacharya was a drone-automation workshop held in January, 2020. This was held by the Aerodynamics Club of Bits Goa. It was a two day long workshop. On the first day we had a guest lecture where the speaker spoke about his journey and his enthusiasm towards aviation. On the next day, first half was completely spent on understanding the physics of how it works and basic terminologies. In the second half, we assemmbled all the parts. I did soldering for the first time then. Once the drone was ready, we automated the drone. Seniors were a great help throughout.
